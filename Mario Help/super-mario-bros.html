About the Movie

Overview

The Super Mario Bros Movie is a 2023 animated film based on the popular video game franchise. It follows the adventures of Mario and Luigi as they navigate the Mushroom Kingdom to rescue Princess Peach from Bowser.

Main Characters

  • Mario: The main protagonist and hero of the story.
  • Luigi: Mario's younger brother and sidekick.
  • Princess Peach: The damsel in distress who needs rescuing.
  • Bowser: The main antagonist and kidnapper of Princess Peach.

Plot Summary

In the movie, Mario and Luigi embark on a quest to rescue Princess Peach from Bowser's clutches. Along the way, they encounter various challenges and make new friends, including Toad and Donkey Kong. The film is filled with action, humor, and heartwarming moments as the brothers learn the importance of teamwork and bravery.
